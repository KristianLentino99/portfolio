# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

The primary audience is founders, investors, technology leaders, recruiters, and potential collaborators evaluating Kristian's engineering depth and founder experience. A secondary audience is comics industry partners and readers discovering the story behind Koomy.

## Product Purpose

Present Kristian Lentino as an engineer-founder who combines production rigor, frontier AI experience, and the persistence required to bootstrap Koomy. Success means a visitor quickly understands the breadth of his work, remembers the combination of engineering rigor and shonen energy, and starts a relevant conversation.

## Positioning

Kristian ships ambitious software with engineering rigor and founder-level ownership, powered by the same long-horizon energy he finds in manga.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: start a conversation through email or LinkedIn.
- Secondary CTA: explore the Koomy story and live product.
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: "I build software with engineering rigor and shonen energy."
- Belief ladder: Kristian is a credible engineer; he can ship real products; he operates with founder ownership; Koomy proves he can build a company and market; he is worth speaking with.
- Proof on hand: current roles at commercetools and Koomy, prior Moneyfarm experience, Scala Matsuri speaking experience, Koomy reader and catalog metrics, publisher partnerships, and live links to Koomy and LinkedIn.

## Brand Personality

Confident, energetic, and rigorous. The voice is first-person founder-speak with selective manga references, grounded claims, and a little irreverence. The intended emotional response is confidence first, followed by curiosity and delight.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic corporate portfolios, recruiter-template minimalism, noisy comic cosplay, decorative manga art without purpose, soft SaaS gradients, glassmorphism, and timid neutral layouts. Comic energy must remain seasoning around credible professional content.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with a clear professional claim, then earn the playful details.
2. Show evidence through real roles, products, metrics, and milestones.
3. Treat the career as a coherent saga without turning it into a gimmick.
4. Make Koomy orange unmistakable and disciplined.
5. Keep every interaction fast, accessible, and intentional.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, semantic landmarks, sufficient contrast in both themes, screen-reader labels, responsive reflow, and reduced-motion preferences.
